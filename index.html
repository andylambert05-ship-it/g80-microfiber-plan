G80 M3 — Microfiber Inventory Plan

Isle of Man Green BMW M3 G80  ·  Ceramic Coated  ·  Cross-referenced from the 21-Step Bi-Weekly Wash Plan v2.0

😖 DEDICATED “DIRTY JOB” TOWELS — Keep Separate Forever

1. Bug Removal Towels — Step 2 (CarPro Bug Out) BUGS

Use: Agitate Bug Out on dry front bumper, hood edge, lower windshield, and mirrors. Treat as consumables — heavy contamination, wash or discard separately.

  • Product: Autofiber Utility 350 — 16”x16” 350gsm (10-pack)
  • Qty: 4–6 towels  ·  Color code: Red / Orange
  • Why: Low pile, affordable, expendable. Zero risk of cross-contaminating paint or coating towels.

2. Tar Removal Towels — Step 3 (CarPro Tar X) TAR ONLY

Use: Wipe Tar X residue off lower rockers, rear bumper, door bottoms on dry surface. Tar stains permanently — treat as fully dedicated consumables.

  • Product: Autofiber Utility 350 — 16”x16” 350gsm (10-pack)
  • Qty: 4–6 towels  ·  Color code: Black (visually distinct from everything else)
  • Why: Same budget workhorse — retire frequently once tarred.

🟡 WHEEL & TIRE TOWELS — Keep Separate from Paint

3. Wheel Face / Overflow Wipe Towels — Steps 4–6 (Koch Chemie MWC) WHEELS

Use: Wipe wheel faces and any iron decon chemical overflow after MWC agitation with EZ Detail brushes and Autofiber Barrel Blade. Never near paint.

  • Product: Autofiber Utility 350 — 16”x16” 350gsm (10-pack)
  • Qty: 4–6 towels  ·  Color code: Yellow

🟢 DOOR JAMB & APC TOWELS

4. Door Jamb Wipe-Down Towels — Step 8 (AllClean / MultiX) JAMBS

Use: After leaf-blower blast, wipe flat surfaces of door jambs post-APC agitation. Touch APC residue — keep separate from paint towels.

  • Primary: Autofiber Mr. Everything Jr. — 12”x12” 390gsm (edgeless) — smaller size for tight jamb spaces
  • Alternative: Autofiber Utility 350 — 16”x16”
  • Qty: 4 towels  ·  Color code: Purple

🔵 PAINT CONTACT TOWELS — Most Critical Category

Key Rule: Per the wash plan, paint towel contact occurs only at Step 15 (Reload 2.0 application & buff-off) and Step 21 (QD final pass). Zero towel contact on paint after rinsing — blower drying is mandatory on all painted surfaces. These towels must be edgeless, lint-free, and ultrasoft.

5. Reload 2.0 — Application / Spread Towels — Step 15 RELOAD – SPREAD

Use: 3–4 sprays of Reload 2.0 onto towel, spread evenly panel-by-panel, wait 30–60 sec to flash. Low-pile 70/30 for even spread without dragging on ION Green metallic.

  • Primary: Autofiber Korean Quadrant Wipe — 16”x16” 350gsm (edgeless, coating-specific) — quadrant fold gives 4 clean faces per towel, designed for ceramic leveling
  • Alternative: Autofiber Korean Pearl 300 — 16”x16” (edgeless, made in S. Korea) — ultra-fine pearl weave, slightly lower pile
  • Qty: 6–8 towels per wash session (one per panel on a full G80 M3)

6. Reload 2.0 — Buff-Off Towels — Step 15 RELOAD – BUFF OFF

Use: Second clean dry towel to buff off the hazy Reload flash (after 30–60 sec). Needs a firm base that resists collapsing during buffing, ultra-fine pile for zero marring.

  • Primary: Autofiber Mr. Everything Jr. — 12”x12” 390gsm (edgeless, ZeroEdge) — stiff base resists collapse, 70/30 ultra-fine pile UV-tested for zero micro-marring
  • Qty: 6–8 towels per session (match count to spread towels)

7. QD / Spot Detailer Final Pass — Step 21 QD ONLY

Use: Final inspection walk-around in direct sunlight at raking angle, spot-correcting any water spots, missed panels, or glass haze on cool, clean, dry paint.

  • Product: Autofiber Mr. Everything Jr. — 12”x12” 390gsm (edgeless)
  • Qty: 2–3 towels — store in a clean sealed bag between washes

⚪ GLASS TOWELS — Interior & Exterior Must Be Kept Separate

8. Exterior Glass Towels — Step 17 (BH Trace-Less / Autofinesse Crystal) GLASS – EXT

Use: Clean exterior windshield, rear glass, side windows. Waffle-weave for the cleaning wipe pass; dry or suede side for streak-free buff.

  • Primary: Autofiber No Streak Freak — 16”x16” 400gsm Waffle Weave (MicroEdge) — industry-proven, no polyester stitching to streak
  • Alternative (2-in-1 flip design): Autofiber Flip GPT — 8.5”x8.5” 1000gsm 3-pack — long pile scrubs, flip to short pile for streak-free buff; 4 clean sections per towel
  • Qty: 4–6 towels  ·  NEVER cross-use with interior glass towels

9. Interior Glass Towels — Step 18 (Invisible Glass Reach & Clean Tool) GLASS – INT

Use: Spray onto MF head of Reach & Clean Tool (not directly on glass). First pass damp to clean; swap to fresh face for dry buff pass. Separate cloths for side windows.

  • Primary: Autofiber Flip GPT — 8.5”x8.5” 1000gsm 3-pack — compact size fits the Reach & Clean tool head perfectly; flip design always gives a fresh dry buffing face
  • Qty: 4–6 towels  ·  NEVER cross-use with exterior glass towels

🟠 DRYING TOWELS — Trim, Mirrors & Non-Paint Surfaces Only

Reminder: The wash plan mandates zero towel contact on paint after rinsing — all paint drying is touchless via leaf blower. These towels only touch mirror housings, badge gaps, trim, exhaust outlets, and jamb areas after the blower has done its work.

10. Non-Paint Drying Towels — Step 14 Overflow DRY – TRIM ONLY

Use: Chase residual water from mirror housings, badge gaps, exhaust tips, panel edges — only after leaf blower. Never drag across clear coat.

  • Primary (Autofiber): Autofiber Amphibian — 20”x30” 1100gsm (Twist/Plush dual-layer, Hidden Edge) — 1100gsm absorbency, hidden internal edge
  • Alternative (Adams): Adam’s Great White Microfiber Drying Towel — waffle weave, highly rated for absorbent blotting
  • Qty: 1–2 towels

⬛ EXHAUST TIP TOWELS

11. Exhaust Tip Cleaning — Step 16 (P21S Polishing Soap) EXHAUST

Use: Apply P21S Polishing Soap with circular motions on each exhaust tip to cut carbon soot and oxidation. These will go black — completely separate from all other towels.

  • Product: Autofiber Mr. Everything Jr. — 12”x12” 390gsm — stiff base ideal for the light scrubbing motion; cheap enough to replace when black
  • Qty: 2–4 towels  ·  Color code: Dark / Black
